# 🇸🇩 Sudan Smart Market

An open-source AI-powered digital marketplace built to empower individuals and small businesses across Sudan.

## 🌍 Vision

To create Sudan's most trusted open-source marketplace, connecting buyers and sellers through modern technology, artificial intelligence, and community collaboration.

## 🎯 Project Goal

Sudan Smart Market aims to solve challenges in digital commerce by providing an accessible platform where individuals, entrepreneurs, and small businesses can promote products and services online.

## ✨ Features

- 🤖 AI-powered product search
- 🛍️ Buy & Sell marketplace
- 🏪 Business profiles
- 💬 In-app communication
- ⭐ Ratings and reviews
- 📍 Location-based search
- 🌐 Arabic & English support
- 🚀 Future AI business assistant tools

## 🛠 Technology

- Google AI Studio
- Firebase
- GitHub
- Artificial Intelligence Integration
- Modern Web Technologies
